The Curated Experience

Journey through the vertical landscape of Italy's most storied coastline. This curated expedition transcends the typical tourist trail, offering private access to cliffside lemon groves, after-hours tours of Ravello's historic villas, and sunset sails on a restored vintage mahogany speedboat.

Your home for the week alternates between a 17th-century converted monastery and a boutique hotel where every balcony frames the iconic pastel pyramid of Positano. Every meal is an editorial on Mediterranean flavors, from Michelin-starred terrace dining to clandestine trattorias known only to locals.
